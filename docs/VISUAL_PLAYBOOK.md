STUNNING VISUAL PLAYBOOK
[Palette — pastel candy, never pure black/#FF]: grass top #7CC24E→#8FD05C with ±4% lightness tile jitter; dirt strata sides #9C6B43/#7A5233 (diorama edge like a cut cake: grass/soil/stone layers); paths cream #E8D5A8→terracotta #D9A36A, ~2 stops lighter than grass, 1px darker rim at path/grass border; water pastel cyan #5BC8DC shallow/#3A9EC9 deep with white foam ring at shores; canopy two greens #5FAE49 + #84C862 top highlight; accents candy pink #F5A8C0, buttercup #F7D75E; sky zenith #4FA8E8 → horizon #BDE3F5; darkest allowed shadow tone ≈ #3A4A5A blue-gray.
[Voxel charm — bake into vertex colors, zero runtime cost]: (1) per-face tinting: top ×1.0, N/S ×0.85, E/W ×0.75, bottom ×0.6 — the 3-value read is the core voxel aesthetic; (2) baked per-vertex AO with the 0fps formula (ao = side1&&side2 ? 0 : 3-(side1+side2+corner), color ×(0.55+0.45·ao/3), flip quad diagonal when ao00+ao11>ao01+ao10); (3) height color ramp: top of world +8% lightness/warmer hue, bottom −8%/cooler; (4) RoundedBoxGeometry (three/addons, radius .06) ONLY for hero props/characters — never terrain chunks.
[Lighting]: HemisphereLight(sky #BFD9F2, ground #E8C9A0, 0.6-0.8) — warm from below, cool from above, this is "cozy"; sun DirectionalLight(#FFF2D9, ~1.2-1.6) elevation ~50° from screen-left-front; shadows NEVER black (hemisphere fills them blue); PCFSoftShadowMap, shadow camera frustum TIGHTENED to the diorama bounds (beats resolution), mapSize 2048 desktop/1024 mid, bias -0.0005, normalBias 0.02-0.05; static sun → renderer.shadowMap.autoUpdate=false + needsUpdate once. Characters: castShadow=false + BLOB shadow (radial-gradient CanvasTexture plane, α~0.35, depthWrite false, polygonOffset, scales/fades with hop height) — the Animal Crossing read.
[Post chain — EffectComposer, importmap-safe, order matters]: RenderPass → UnrealBloomPass at HALF resolution (strength 0.25-0.4, radius 0.6-0.8, threshold 0.85 — only sparkles/lanterns bloom) → HorizontalTiltShiftShader + VerticalTiltShiftShader ShaderPasses (h=v≈2.5/dimension, r≈0.45-0.55 focus band on the player — THE miniature-diorama signature; use these, NOT BokehPass, on ortho) → one combined custom grade+vignette ShaderPass (contrast 1.05, lift 0.01, saturation 1.15, warm tint ×(1.03,1.0,0.97), corners darkened ~25% via smoothstep vignette) → FXAA (mobile) or SMAAPass (desktop) → OutputPass. Renderer: ACESFilmicToneMapping, exposure 1.1.
[Sky/atmosphere]: gradient sky DOME (SphereGeometry BackSide ShaderMaterial, mix(bottom,top,pow(max(dir.y,0),0.6)), fog:false, renderOrder -1) — not flat scene.background; 8-16 puffy clouds = merged squashed icosahedrons in ONE InstancedMesh, flat white, drifting slowly AND castShadow=true (drifting cloud shadows = huge "alive" signal for free). SKIP standard THREE.Fog — it does nothing/greys uniformly on small ortho dioramas (three.js #4861); atmosphere comes from the sky gradient + vignette.
[Water]: subdivided plane (64×64 desktop), toon/lambert #5BC8DC flatShading, onBeforeCompile vertex bob = two offset sines (amp ~0.12); foam = white band painted on SHORE geometry at water level via smoothstep on worldY synced to the bob (no depth-texture foam); sparkle = fragment specular threshold (smoothstep(0.97,1,dot(n,sunDir))) so flat-shaded facets flash and the bloom pass catches them; two-color depth mix by distance-to-shore.
[Motion — nothing perfectly still]: wind sway on ALL foliage via onBeforeCompile (height-masked quadratic: sin(t·1.8 + worldX·.5 + worldZ·.7)·0.08 + gust sin(t·3.7)·0.02 — world-position phase desyncs instances free); rotation springs on props (vel += (target-rot)·80·dt, vel ×= 0.9) for juicy bumps; squash-and-stretch hop (scaleY 1+0.3sin, scaleXZ 1/√scaleY); fireflies 40-80 THREE.Points additive depthWrite:false warm #FFDD88 twinkling (feeds bloom), drifting petals 100-200 normal blending, 4-point star sparkle sprites on interactions; slow cloud drift; 2-4s idle bob on creatures.
[Quality tiers — detect coarse pointer + WEBGL_debug_renderer_info, then ADAPT by measuring avg frame time over first 120 frames, >26ms = drop a tier]: HIGH = DPR≤2, 2048 shadows, full post; MID = DPR≤1.5, 1024 shadows, bloom quarter-res 0.2, FXAA; LOW = DPR 1, NO EffectComposer at all (direct render — composer copies cost even when passes are cheap), blob shadows only, CSS radial-gradient overlay div as the free vignette, keep ACES + baked vertex charm (the scene must still read 80% as good — that is WHY charm is baked into geometry, not passes). DPR is always the first lever (2→1.5 = −44% pixels).
[Composition]: ~60% open space / 40% content; props clustered in 3s (tree+rock+flowers); paths curve, never straight >6 tiles; one focal structure per zone; sparse is on-style (Pokopia reviewers praise its subdued moments).
